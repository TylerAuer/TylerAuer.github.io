Super_Bowl_Props

Live source

Front-end app to track a game during each year's Super Bowl. Each year, nearly 100 people try to make predicitons about the Super Bowl. They guess the final score, who will win, the length of the National Anthem, the color of the Gatorade poured on the winning coach, and 40+ other props. I track the outcomes in a Google Sheet and the site updates automatically through the Google Drive API
